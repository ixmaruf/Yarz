Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Introduction

YARZ ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you place an order, including:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Delivery address
  • City and area information

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information is automatically collected, including your browser type, device type, and browsing patterns. We use localStorage to store your cart items and session preferences locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders (delivery updates, confirmations)
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Store your preferences for a better shopping experience

Data Storage

Your order data is securely stored in our Google Sheets database. Personal information stored locally on your device (via localStorage) can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Courier services (for order delivery)
  • Payment processors (when applicable)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your shopping cart, user preferences, and session data. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server unless you place an order.
